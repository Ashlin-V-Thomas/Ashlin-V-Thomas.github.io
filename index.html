About me

I am an Integrated MSc. student at the National Institute of Science Education and Research(NISER) Bhubaneswar, India. Currently, I am pursuing a major degree in physics and a minor degree in mathematics; and have completed two years of the course.

Raised in the serene countryside of Kerala, my curiosity was sparked by the mathematical descriptions of natural phenomena.
